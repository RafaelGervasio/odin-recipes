Old School Mac n' Cheese

Description

This is a completely unpretentious, down-home macaroni and cheese recipe just like my grandma and mom always made.

A simple white sauce where you add cheese and a few other flavors with macaroni and a potato chip crust.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 pounds whole-wheat macaroni
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 6 cups milk, divided
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 3 (8 ounce) packages shredded American cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) bag potato chips (such as Lay'sÂ®), crushed
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • butter-flavored cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water
  3. Stir Worcestershire sauce, mustard powder, onion powder, and cayenne pepper into the mixture; season with salt and black pepper.
  4. Cook sauce, whisking frequently, until it begins to thicken, about 10 minutes. Add about half the package of shredded Cheddar cheese; stir continually until the cheese melts completely.
  5. Stir drained macaroni into the cheese sauce to coat.
  6. Mix crushed potato chips, 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, and Parmesan cheese in a bowl. Top the macaroni with the potato chip mixture evenly
  7. Bake in preheated oven until the crust is golden brown and the sauce is bubbling, 35 to 45 minutes.
